Discover the main life events that made me who I am in chronological order.

1990

2002

Largely due to the 1998-2002 Argentine great depression
2003
As an adolescent I spent a lot of time playing Rugby and became my team's captain.

Rugby taught me the values of Teamwork, Respect, Discipline and Sportsmanship.

I'm extremely curious and I love science.

I've always loved to learn new things, mainly in the realm of the sciences. My favorite podcast is The Skeptic's Guide to the Universe.

2012

2014
